## Platform-Specific Content Frameworks

### Reddit: The Authority Building Engine

Reddit rewards authenticity over promotion with a proven **90% value, 10% promotion** formula. Success requires understanding community psychology and anti-promotional content structures.

**25 High-Performance Templates:**

**Problem-Solution Framework:**

```
Title: "[Specific Problem] - Here's what actually worked after trying everything"
Structure:
- Brief context of struggle (vulnerability builds trust)
- 3-4 failed attempts with specific details
- The solution that worked (step-by-step)
- Results with proof
- Offer to answer questions

```

**Behind-the-Scenes Breakdown:**

```
Title: "I [achieved specific result] - Here's the step-by-step breakdown"
Structure:
- Quick credibility statement
- Exact process (numbered steps)
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Resources mentioned
- Transparent about challenges

```

**Crowd-Sourced Wisdom:**

```
Title: "What's the best [specific thing] you've discovered for [problem]?"
Structure:
- Context for why you're asking
- Your current approach
- Specific criteria you're looking for
- Promise to compile/share results

```

**The Anti-Promotion Strategy:**

- Lead with problem identification first
- Share your struggle/research process
- Present solution with specific steps
- Include failures and what didn't work
- Only mention products/services if directly relevant to solution

**50+ Reddit-Optimized Hooks:**

- "After failing at [X] for [timeframe], I finally figured out..."
- "Everyone told me [common advice] was key, but..."
- "I wasted [amount] on [solution] before discovering..."
- "Most people get [topic] completely wrong..."
- "The uncomfortable truth about [topic] that no one talks about..."

**Subreddit-Specific Adaptation Guidelines:**

- **r/entrepreneur**: Data-driven posts with specific metrics, humble bragging acceptable
- **r/personalfinance**: Educational focus, real scenarios, avoid product recommendations
- **r/careerguidance**: Experience-based insights, supportive tone, industry context
- **r/marketing**: Strategy over tools, case studies, trend discussions

3. **Payoff (30-60s)**: Satisfy curiosity/provide promised value

YouTube Shorts algorithm operates on an "Explore-Exploit" system, testing content with seed audiences before scaling to broader reach.

3. **Engagement Metrics** - Likes, comments, shares within 24-72 hours
