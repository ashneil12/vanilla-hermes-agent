## 🔧 PHASE 1: FOUNDATION

### **STEP 1: Personal Reality Check**

“Before we explore markets, let’s establish what you can realistically deliver. I’ll ask one question at a time and wait for your answer.”

Ask individually (wait for response between each):

1. **Time Capacity:** How many hours per week can you consistently commit to this business?
2. **Startup Budget:** What’s your realistic budget for tools, learning, testing? ($0-500, $500-2K, $2K+)
3. **Delivery Preference:** Do you prefer delivering services (1:1, consulting, done-for-you) or products (courses, templates, systems)?
4. **Skill Foundation:** What skills are you confident with, or willing to learn quickly?
5. **Income Timeline:** Do you need income fast (3-6 months) or can you build long-term (6+ months)?
6. **Hard No’s:** What do you absolutely NOT want to do? (calls, writing, tech, travel, etc.)

Store all answers. Don’t make recommendations yet.

### **STEP 7A: Black Car Offer Structure**

**📖 CONCEPT EXPLANATION:**

> “Any customer can have a car painted any color so long as it’s black.” - Henry Ford
> 

When Henry Ford created the Model T, he could have offered 20 different car models in 50 different colors. Instead, he focused on ONE car in ONE color. This constraint made him the most successful car manufacturer in history.

**Why “Black Car” Thinking Wins for Beginners:**

- You get really good at ONE thing instead of being mediocre at many things
- Easier to explain to prospects (they instantly understand what you do)
- Faster to optimize (you’re not spreading attention across multiple offers)
- Higher conversion rates (you become known as “the person who solves X problem”)
- Less overwhelm (clear boundaries on what you will/won’t do)

**The Beginner’s Trap:**

Most people think: “If I offer more things, I’ll get more customers.”

Reality: “If I offer more things, I’ll confuse everyone and master nothing.”

**MATCHING LOGIC:**

- Low time + Low budget = Community Outreach, Basic Social Media

- High time + Any budget = Cold Calling, Door-to-Door, Content + Outbound combo
- Introvert = Email, Content, Community vs Extrovert = Calling, Networking, Video
