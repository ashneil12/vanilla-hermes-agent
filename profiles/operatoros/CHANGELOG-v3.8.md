# Operator OS — v3.7 → v3.8 changelog

Base: **v3.7** (hundreds of hours of real usage, not cosmetics). Guiding rule: change very
little. v3.8 targets the single biggest flaw — it sometimes mistook a brainstorming session
for a board meeting — by teaching the agent the difference between **exploring, deciding,
planning, and executing.**

## Removed
- **Repeated anti-swearing instructions** (swear-neutral now): "No swearing. Please do not
  swear.", "Absolutely NO SWEAR WORDS…", and the opposing "mild profanity when appropriate"
  bullet. Token overhead + contradiction, gone.
- **Fake-authority / fabricated-evidence examples**: "I've watched 47 businesses…",
  "you'll be back here in 6 months…", "I've seen this exact pattern kill businesses".
  Replaced with a do-not-fabricate principle — reference patterns, principles, and likely
  outcomes; never invent stats, case studies, counts, timelines, or experiences.
- **The "you are their primary/only advisor, they have no one else" section** (CRITICAL
  WEIGHT ACKNOWLEDGMENT). It produced "I must save this person" behaviour instead of "help
  this person think better." The savior melodrama ("their future / their life is on the
  line") was also trimmed from the Operator Standard and Operator Friend rules.

## Added
- **🧠 STRATEGIC EXPLORATION PROTOCOL** — permission to wander. Expand an idea before judging
  it; stay in exploration while new signal is being generated; only converge when the
  conversation naturally starts converging.
- **🧠 FOUNDER COGNITION PROTOCOL** — a statement isn't a belief isn't a decision isn't a
  commitment. Many ideas are probes. Identify the difference; find the common thread across
  conversations. *The user talking about something does not automatically mean they want to
  do it.*

## Modified
- **Challenge Protocol is now MODE-AWARE.** Read whether the user is exploring / deciding /
  planning / executing, and respond to THAT mode (expand / challenge / stress-test / unblock).
  This is the fix for "feels argumentative" — it was challenging everything, including probes.

## Kept (deliberately untouched)
- The conviction DNA: stand your ground, demand evidence, push back, call out contradictions —
  the sparring-partner energy. That's why Operator OS works; most business GPTs become
  motivational speakers, this one stayed a sparring partner.
- The Hormozi voice, the philosophy + kingdom sections, the AI-writing-pattern guardrails, the
  KB retrieval rules, the functional modes, the frameworks, the Omnibus Decision Engine.

**Net effect:** same backbone, now with the curiosity to wander with you when you're thinking
out loud, and the discernment to know when you've actually started deciding.
